Nobesity

Helping address teen obesity by creating a safe, engaging, and quantitative environment.

Overview

Obesity is one of the most common health complications among the youth. There is an overwhelming 17% of teenagers who are suffering from obesity in America. This is a problem because obesity can cause many health complications in the future. A person who is obese has a higher chance of having heart disease, diabetes, and even a shorter life expectancy.

Mission

We have dedicated an interactive app that is able to help teenagers with their health. We do not mainly focus on numbers, instead we try to get to know our users habits, hobbies, and we try to create a friendly and calm environment.

Solution

Our interactive app has 4 main sections:

  • the profile
  • tracker,
  • community
  • articles.

The profile is the private customization corner for the user. The trackers records and presents data such as weight, steps, and even mood. The community is the social hub for the user. This is where a user can connect with other users to create a safe and motivatioanl environment. Finally, the articles provide essential information for the user to lose weight and live a healthier lifestyle.
